How It Works

Step One

You simply order the #of races you'll need for your event. You can order anywhere from just ONE race and up to SIX races with anywhere from 4 to 18 horses in a race!

Step Two

You'll submit a form to complete with the name of the race & the horse's names, too! Your videos will be delivered online to use and via Dropbox.

Step Three

After that, you log in here to create a 'Virtual Sportsbook' for your guests. You'll enter the race & horse names, decide what bets are available to the players, fill in the opening odds and choose if you want 'fixed' odds for your race or live odds. You also get to decide the player's starting bankroll for your derby night, too!

Step Four

The player get a QR code where they can log in and place their wagers on all the races. When a race is over, you update the race with the top 3 finishers and Derby Knight will then calculate everyone's balance and a leaderboard to see how everyone has fared!
